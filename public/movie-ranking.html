The Best Movies According to Ralph

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

The Hundred-Foot Journey

It shows a lot of points about perseverance and motivation. Shows that failing plays a big part to being successful.

Chef

Provides a lesson to those who have crumbled and started to think about quitting. Also, shows that whatever happens, your family will always be there for you.

Burnt

Arrogance takes you to places that you've never explored. Be cautious at all times, and learn to be humble all the time.
